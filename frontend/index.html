How RootsLink Works

A four-step AI-driven pathway from talent to opportunity.

Step 1

Build Your Profile

Share your skills, goals, location, and barriers. The system encodes your context into a structured profile.

Step 2

Get Matched

Our algorithms score every opportunity and mentor against your profile — prioritising fit, feasibility, and local impact.

Step 3

Follow Your Pathway

Receive a step-by-step pathway — not just a list of links, but a sequenced journey toward your goals.

Step 4

Grow and Give Back

As you progress, your matches improve. Alumni become mentors. The network compounds over time.

The Intelligence Behind the Platform

Four scoring algorithms working in parallel.

Algorithm 1

Opportunity Fit Score

Matches your skills, interests, eligibility, location, and deadline readiness against every opportunity.

Algorithm 2

Mentor Match Score

Ranks mentors by field alignment, regional relevance, language, lived experience, and capacity.

Algorithm 3

Brain-Drain Risk Index

Estimates your structural vulnerability — aspiration-opportunity mismatch, mentorship scarcity, and local pathway visibility.

Algorithm 4

Retention Priority Score

Re-ranks opportunities to favour those that maximise local contribution alongside personal growth.
